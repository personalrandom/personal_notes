To make 3 buns :
    45 g whole milk cold 
    7g sugar (1 + 1/2 teaspoon)
    2.5 g instant dry yeast (3/4 teaspoon)
    3 g salt (1/2 teaspoon)
    1 whole egg
	
    150 g bread flour
	
    56 g butter
	
put 1 on stand mixer and whisk with whisker manually
add 2 and mix for 10 minutes on low speed (1)

gave me 280g dough
	93g per dough

make this recipe using this guy's method : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s34RoYJNDuc
	https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=adkZ8IQqq4Q

Egg wash before baking
    1 whole egg
    15 ml water

bake for 20-25 minutes at 160C fan on
